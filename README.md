# ☕ Indian Tea Industry Performance Analysis
A Power BI project analyzing the performance of the Indian tea industry, focusing on production, revenue, exports, pricing trends, and profitability.

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes the performance of the Indian tea industry using Power BI.  
It focuses on production, revenue, exports, pricing, and profitability across states, regions, tea types, and estates.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
The Tea Board of India requires a unified system to analyze tea production, exports, revenue, and pricing across regions and states.  
However, fragmented data makes it difficult to identify trends and support timely decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze tea production across different states  
- Compare regional production and revenue performance  
- Evaluate tea type contribution to production and revenue  
- Analyze export trends over time  
- Study the relationship between price and revenue  
- Identify top-performing tea estates  

---

## 🛠  Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Microsoft Excel  
- Data Visualization  
- Data Analysis   

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Kerala and West Bengal consistently perform as top production states  
- Green Tea and Nilgiri Tea contribute significantly to revenue  
- Export trends show steady growth after 2021  
- A strong positive relationship exists between price and revenue  
- Leading estates such as Darjeeling Hills and Assam Valley drive major contributions
